# Glossary of Terms

`Political Party`
:   A political party is a formally recognized political organization active with candidates and/or elected officials 
    being represented within a governing body. In the US, most political parties have a national office with state and
    county committees carrying out the work of defining political platforms, registering voters, recruiting and running
    candidates.
